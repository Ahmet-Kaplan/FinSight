Recommended outline:

# Industry Research Report

## Executive Summary
Writing guidance: Summarize the core thesis, key evidence, and the base/bull/bear outlook in plain investor language.

## Scope and Methodology
Writing guidance: Define industry boundaries, data windows, regions covered, and scenario assumptions.

## Market Structure and Size
Writing guidance: Quantify current market size, historical growth, and regional split; include TAM/SAM/SOM where available.

## Value Chain and Economics
Writing guidance: Break down value capture across upstream, midstream, and downstream segments with margin and pricing context.

## Demand Drivers
Writing guidance: Analyze end-market demand by segment, cyclicality, and leading indicators.

## Supply, Capacity, and Utilization
Writing guidance: Evaluate capacity additions, utilization trends, bottlenecks, and inventory dynamics.

## Competitive Landscape
Writing guidance: Compare incumbents and challengers on share, strategy, differentiation, and execution quality.

## Policy, Regulation, and Geopolitics
Writing guidance: Assess policy incentives, export controls, compliance burdens, and regional strategic risks.

## Technology Roadmap and Disruption Risk
Writing guidance: Explain major technology shifts, adoption timelines, and potential disruption to incumbents.

## 3-Year Scenario Outlook
Writing guidance: Provide explicit base/bull/bear assumptions and outputs for growth, pricing, margins, and valuation bands.

## Risks and Catalysts
Writing guidance: List the highest-impact downside risks and upside catalysts with objective signposts.

## Conclusion
Writing guidance: State a clear investor-oriented conclusion and what to monitor next.
